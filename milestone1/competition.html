Who are my competitors?

My competitors are the offical websites for games. Not only that, but I also have competitors like wikipedia and google because those also contain the info I'm presenting.

Some of my competitors are far more offical and known. Take google, they've been around for decades.

What are they doing wrong?

None of those sources have the in-depth experience that I have because I play the games I review and add dates to. They are slightly soulless when it comes to the games because they lack the gaming experience.

What can I do better?
